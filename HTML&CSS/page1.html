USA Travels

I'm Chris, I'm starting this website as both a practice for my HTML, CSS and Javascript skills, as well as a way to write down and record all the fantastic times I've had with my partner, Rebecca. Recently we returned from a trip to the USA.
We decided the best way to lay it all out is day by day and there's 16 of 'em!
The days kind of bleed into each other. Reason for this is every trip would start in one country/state and end in another! Time travel is involved a lot as well.
Not in a weird Dr Who way, just normal timezone way. What did you think I meant?

The first place that comes to mind is the but we'll cover all of them in each section. In total we went to four different and wonderful places:

  • New York
  • San Francisco
  • Honolulu
  • Los Angeles

This trip changed us and ignited a desire to travel. This website is a log of our adventures in the above places, and we will add more entries as we go further afield.
